# Privacy Policy — Everest Focus

**Last updated:** May 2026

## Overview

Everest Focus is a productivity timer and watch face for Wear OS. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.**

All data generated by the app (focus sessions, scores, streaks, preferences) is stored locally on your watch device using Android DataStore and a local Room database. No data leaves your device.

## Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored on-device only:

- Focus session timestamps and durations
- Interruption counts per session
- Focus scores (calculated locally)
- Daily/weekly aggregated statistics
- User preferences (timer durations, theme selection, toggles)
- Streak tracking (consecutive days of use)

Free users retain 7 days of history. Pro users retain 30 days. Older data is automatically deleted.

## Calendar Access

If you enable "Calendar Awareness," the app reads your device calendar to display upcoming events and suggest shorter focus sessions. Calendar data is read-only and never stored or transmitted.

## Notification Access

If you enable "Auto Do Not Disturb," the app accesses the notification policy to toggle Do Not Disturb during focus sessions. No notification content is read or stored.

## Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Google Play. We do not collect or store payment information.

## Third-Party Services

The app uses:
- **Google Play Billing** — for processing in-app purchases (governed by Google's privacy policy)

No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are included.

## Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
