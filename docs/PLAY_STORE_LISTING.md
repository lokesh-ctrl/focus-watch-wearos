## Full Description

The only watch face that tracks your focus.

Focus Dial turns your Wear OS watch into a productivity companion. No app to open, no phone required — just tap the dial to start a focus session and watch your progress arc fill in real time.

INTELLIGENT FOCUS TIMER
• Customizable Pomodoro sessions (15-120 minutes)
• Configurable break durations (3-30 minutes)
• Set a daily goal and track sessions with visual dots
• Focus score rates each session based on completion and interruptions

ALWAYS-ON PRODUCTIVITY
• See focus state, elapsed time, and progress directly on your watch face
• Ambient mode shows time + current focus status
• 6 watch face complications for at-a-glance stats
• Tap the dial to start or stop — zero friction

SMART FEATURES
• Calendar awareness — shows upcoming meetings, suggests shorter sessions
• Streak tracking — build daily consistency
• Interruption counter — know how focused you really were
• Weekly stats and focus history

PRO FEATURES
• 4 beautiful themes (Minimal, Ember, Ocean, Monochrome)
• Adaptive breaks — automatically extends rest after 3+ sessions or late hours
• Auto Do Not Disturb — silences notifications during focus
• 30-day session history (vs 7-day free)
• All 6 complications unlocked

PRIVACY FIRST
• All data stays on your watch — no accounts, no cloud, no tracking
• No ads, ever

Built for developers, students, remote workers, and anyone who values deep work. The lowest-friction focus trigger that exists.
